About WeekNab

WeekNab was born from a simple realization: most of us live and spend on a weekly basis, but budget on a monthly one. This disconnect makes it hard to make good financial decisions day-to-day.

Our application bridges this gap by transforming your YNAB monthly budget into a weekly format, providing you with the insights you need to stay on track throughout the month.

Built with Vue.js and integrated with the YNAB API, WeekNab is designed to be a complementary tool for existing YNAB users who want more granular control over their weekly spending.
